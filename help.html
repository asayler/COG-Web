Help!

We're from the CS Department, and we're here to help.

President Ronald Reagan on Aug. 12, 1986

COG is still in beta. As such, you may encounter occasional bugs. Or maybe you just need to know how to use the system. Either way, feel free to email any issues you encounter or questions you have to Andy at andy.sayler@colorado.edu. Please include a description of you issue and the browser/OS you are using.

If Andy gets hit by a bus, then please email help@cs.colorado.edu instead. In addition, should you feel so inclined, you are welcome to file bug reports or feature requests directly on GitHub. You know, if that's your thing... Bug reports sent via carrier pigeon should conform to RFC 1149. All feature requests will be considered relative to their merit and the size of their attached bribes.

COG-Web Beta: Code and license available on GitHub. Patches, bug reports, and feature requests welcome. Built by Andy Sayler in Boulder, CO.
