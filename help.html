COG is still in beta. As such, you may encounter occasional bugs. Or maybe you just need to know how to use the system. Either way, feel free to email any issues you encounter or questions you have to {{site.email}}. Please include a description of your issue and the browser/OS you are using.

Should you feel so inclined, you are welcome to file bug reports or feature requests directly on GitHub. You know, if that's your thing... Bug reports sent via carrier pigeon should conform to RFC 1149. All feature requests will be considered relative to their merit and the size of their attached bribes.
